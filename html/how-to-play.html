How to Play Secret Hitler

A game of deception, deduction, and betrayal.

Setup

  • Players: 5-10 players.
  • Teams: Players are secretly divided into Liberals and Fascists. One player is secretly the Hitler.
  • Objective: Liberals must pass 5 Liberal policies or stop Hitler. Fascists must pass 6 Fascist policies or get Hitler elected as Chancellor.
  • Materials: Policy tiles, role cards, and a game board.

Gameplay

  1. Assign Roles: Each player receives a secret role card. Liberals do not know each other, but Fascists know who each other are. Hitler only knows they are Hitler unless told otherwise.
  2. Elect a Government: A President is selected. The President nominates a Chancellor, and all players vote on the proposed government. If the vote passes, the government begins its work.
  3. Legislative Session: The President draws 3 policy tiles, discards 1, and passes the remaining 2 to the Chancellor, who discards 1 and enacts the last policy. Policies can be Liberal or Fascist.
  4. Executive Action: After certain Fascist policies are passed, the President gains special powers like investigating roles, calling special elections, or even assassinating a player.

Winning Conditions

  • Liberals: Pass 5 Liberal policies or assassinate Hitler.
  • Fascists: Pass 6 Fascist policies or elect Hitler as Chancellor after 3 Fascist policies are enacted.
