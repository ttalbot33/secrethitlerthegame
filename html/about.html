About Secret Hitler

The origins and history of this thrilling board game.

What is Secret Hitler?

Secret Hitler is a social deduction board game designed for 5-10 players. The game takes players back to 1930s Germany, where they secretly assume roles as either Liberals or Fascists. One player takes on the hidden role of Hitler. The goal? Liberals must enact liberal policies or uncover and stop Hitler, while Fascists work covertly to pass fascist policies and help Hitler gain power.

History

The game was created by Max Temkin, Mike Boxleiter, Tommy Maranges, and Mac Schubert. It was officially released in 2016 after a highly successful Kickstarter campaign. Secret Hitler quickly gained popularity for its unique gameplay and high-quality components, earning critical acclaim and becoming a favorite among fans of strategy and party games.

Why is it so popular?

The game combines strategy, deception, and teamwork, making it an engaging experience for players of all types. Its mechanics encourage lively discussions, alliances, and betrayals, keeping everyone on their toes. Additionally, the game's beautiful artwork and production values add to its charm.
