What's in the Box?

Explore the contents of the Secret Hitler game!

  • 17 Policy Tiles: 11 Fascist policies and 6 Liberal policies to pass during the game.
  • 10 Role Cards: Including Hitler, Fascists, and Liberals for secret role assignment.
  • 10 Party Membership Cards: Divided between Fascist and Liberal memberships to match the role cards.
  • President and Chancellor Plaques: Used to identify the current players in these roles.
  • Election Tracker Board: Tracks failed elections and triggers special events.
  • Game Board: Separate boards for games with 5–6 players and 7–10 players.
  • Rulebook: A comprehensive guide to understanding and playing the game.
