What is Secret Hitler?

Secret Hitler is a social deduction board game for 5-10 players. Players are secretly divided into two teams: the Liberals and the Fascists. The Fascists work together to sow distrust and install their leader, Hitler, while the Liberals must work to uncover the truth and stop them. With hidden roles, voting, and strategic play, each round is full of suspense and excitement.

Gameplay Overview

Players take turns enacting policies through voting while trying to determine who can be trusted. The game is filled with twists, as secret roles and hidden agendas create an intense atmosphere of bluffing and deduction.
